About me

Hi, I am Yulong Wan, a Ph.D. candidate at the Key Laboratory of Speech Acoustics and Content Understanding at Institute of Acoustics (IOA), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). I received my Bachelor of Science degree from School of Electronics Engineering & Computer Science (EECS), as well as my Bachelor of Economics degree (double major) from China Center for Economic Research (CCER), Peking University, China, in 2009. After that, I started to pursue my graduate study at Audio Signal Processing under the supervision of Prof. Yonghong Yan.

Specialties: Music signal processing, voice activity detection, speaker diarization and machine learning.
