About Me

Neverbland is a team of designers, developers and doers.
Specialists in user experience, interaction design and product development.

Having earned our spurs in startups, we understand the importance of simple, elegant solutions and quick releases; matching beautiful aesthetic to functional design.

We're driven by the Web's ability to unite and empower. That's why we're passionate about collaboration and the open source movement.

But it's what we haven't done that really excites us.
